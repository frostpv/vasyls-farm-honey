Thank you for holding a small piece of Vasyl’s Farm.

This jar comes from my first small honey harvest — from two bee families, a garden apiary, and a dream of building a real farm step by step.

About this honey

  • Type: Wildflower honey
  • Harvest: Spring 2026
  • Location: Kyiv region, Ukraine
  • Batch: First small batch
  • Jar size: 100 ml / 200 ml

The exact floral source is mixed, so I call it wildflower honey. It was collected by bees from the plants blooming around the apiary in spring.

The story

I started beekeeping in 2026 with two Ukrainian steppe bee families placed in a small garden in Kyiv region.

It was scary, beautiful, chaotic, and very real. I got stung, made mistakes, learned by doing, and harvested my first small batch of honey.

This jar is not a supermarket product. It is a small gift from the beginning of a much bigger journey.

Follow the journey

Vasyl’s Farm is my long-term project about building a small real farm from scratch: bees, berries, handmade products, experiments, failures, numbers, and progress.
